Which countries are most responsible

Does the answer suprise you?

Now, basically every country on Earth contributes to climate change. Everyone needs power, everyone needs fuel, everyone needs something and that something usually releases pollutants. But which countries have the most impact? You may believe it is the less-developed countries, as they have less resources to access more renewable energy sources, and while this is true, they are not the greatest polluters.

Source. EU (27) is the entire EU.

That would be China, the US, and India. Why? Well, for one, they are the most populous countries. India has the largest population, followed by China and the US. This causes them to require significantly more energy, agricultural output, industry, exports and imports, transportation, infrastructure, and more. All of these factors cause them to produce the most emissions in the world. But, that's not the only reason.
